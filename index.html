Typica Type

American Type Foundry

We design original typefaces and custom lettering with care and intention. Created to serve students, individuals, companies, and organizations. All of our fonts are crafted with the human experience in mind.

Typica was founded in 2025 and our first typefaces will be released late 2025 / early 2026.

We're always open to hearing from both established and emerging designers. If you’re working on a type project or simply have an idea you believe in, we’d love to talk. Our goal is to support and celebrate 21st century American type design.
